A step-by-step guide to becoming a data analyst over the next 5 years. Follow this roadmap to master essential skills, build your portfolio, and achieve your career goals.

Year 1: Fundamentals and Foundations

Master the basics of Excel, SQL, and Python. Lay a strong foundation for data analysis by learning essential tools and concepts.

Year 2: Intermediate Skills Development

Advance your skills in data cleaning, visualization, and statistics. Start building small projects to apply your knowledge.

Year 3: Advanced Analytics and Specialization

Focus on advanced analytics, machine learning basics, and domain-specific expertise. Build projects showcasing advanced techniques.

Year 4: Professional Projects and Networking

Work on large-scale projects, contribute to open-source, and connect with professionals in the field to expand your opportunities.

Year 5: Achieving Your Goal

Polish your portfolio, prepare for interviews, and target remote job opportunities with international companies.
