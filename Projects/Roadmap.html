Data Analyst Roadmap

Here is the roadmap for becoming a data analyst over the next 5 years. This roadmap is structured into yearly, quarterly, and monthly targets to help you systematically achieve your goal.

Yearly Roadmap

  • Year 1: Fundamentals and Foundations
  • Year 2: Intermediate Skills Development
  • Year 3: Advanced Analytics and Specialization
  • Year 4: Professional Projects and Networking
  • Year 5: Targeting International Remote Roles

Quarterly Breakdown

  • Q1: Learn basic tools (Excel, SQL, Python)
  • Q2: Focus on data cleaning and basic statistics
  • Q3: Data visualization and small projects
  • Q4: Build and publish your portfolio

Monthly Tasks

Each month, specific tasks and resources are assigned. For example:

  • Month 1: Excel basics and pivot tables
  • Month 2: SQL queries and database exploration
  • Month 3: Python basics and data manipulation with pandas

Learning Resources

  • Kaggle - Datasets and competitions
  • Coursera - Data analysis courses
  • DataCamp - Python, SQL, and visualization tutorials
  • Khan Academy - Free statistics and probability lessons
