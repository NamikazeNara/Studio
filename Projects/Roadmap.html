Data Analyst Roadmap

Here is the roadmap for becoming a data analyst in the next 5 years.

  • Year 1: Learn basic data analysis tools (Excel, SQL, etc.)
  • Year 2: Learn advanced SQL, Python, and Data Visualization
  • Year 3: Build projects, participate in Kaggle competitions
  • Year 4: Master Machine Learning, Statistical Analysis
  • Year 5: Apply for jobs, grow in your data analyst career
